About Me
I am an Alan and Anne Taetle Early Career Associate Professor at the School of Cybersecurity and Privacy at Georgia Tech. My research interests are in system security and cryptography. I'm interested in both theory and practice with particular interests in side-channel attacks, hardware security, cryptanalysis, secure multiparty computation (MPC), verifiable computation and SNARKS.

I have a PhD in Computer Science from the Technion Israel's Institute of Technology.


I am looking for excellent students interested in working on topics related to security and cryptography
